Using Journal Entry

The Journal Entry page consolidates all your VAT transactions into a professional General Journal with automatic Debit/Credit balancing across five sections: Sales, Purchases, Expenses, Taxes & Licenses, and CAPEX.

Load Transaction Files

Import .vat, .purch/.npurch/.rpurch, .exp/.nexp, .taxes, and .capex files. The system automatically processes each file and posts the appropriate Debit/Credit entries.

Batch Loading

Use dropdown menus to load multiple files at once (e.g., Load All Three for Purchases). Categories are automatically combined.

Automatic Calculations

Calculates Net (Gross ÷ 1.12), Tax (Net × 0.12), Withholding (Net × Rate), and posts to correct accounts.

Editable Debit/Credit Fields

Manually adjust Debit and Credit amounts. Changes automatically recalculate section subtotals and grand totals.

Real-Time Totals

View Total Debits, Total Credits, and the Difference (should be zero for balanced journals).

Print-Ready Journal

Click Print Journal for a clean, print-optimized version.

📊 Supported File Formats

.vat, .purch, .npurch, .rpurch, .exp, .nexp, .taxes, .capex — all JSON-based files from tracker modules.

VAT Calculation Formulas

Net Sales / Net Purchases / Net Expenses

Formula: Net = Gross ÷ 1.12

Example: Gross = ₱1,120.00 → Net = ₱1,000.00
Output Tax / Input Tax

Formula: Tax = Net × 0.12

Example: Net = ₱1,000.00 → Tax = ₱120.00
Withholding Tax

Formula: Withholding = Net × Rate

Example: Net = ₱1,000.00, Rate = 2% → Withholding = ₱20.00

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my work?

Use "Save Data to File" in the left sidebar. Downloads a .vat (Sales), .purch (Purchases), .exp (Expenses), .taxes (Taxes), or .capex (CAPEX) file.

Why can't I export to Excel?

All required company fields must be filled: Company Name, TIN, Address, Line of Business, Authorized Employee.

How do I edit multiple cells at once?

Click "Select Multiple" in the sidebar, then click+drag to select a range. Use Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V, or Delete.

What's the correct TIN format?

000-000-000-000 (12 digits). System auto-formats and validates as you type.

How do I create a complete General Journal?

Use the Journal Entry page (journal_entry.html). Load your transaction files and the system automatically consolidates all transactions into a balanced General Journal.

What happens at high zoom?

At 175%+ headers shorten. At 200%+ less important columns hide. Use Table Focus Mode (Ctrl+Shift+F) for maximum space.

Is my data secure?

All data stored locally in browser or in files you save. No data sent to any server. Works entirely offline.
